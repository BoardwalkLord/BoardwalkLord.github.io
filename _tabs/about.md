## About

I'm Kenneth Wong, based in Malaysia, working toward a career in
cybersecurity with a particular interest in the defensive and
governance side of the field — security operations, incident response,
and how Malaysian businesses navigate their obligations under laws like
the Cyber Security Act 2024 and the PDPA.

This site is where I document that journey. Most of what you'll find
here are learning notes and writeups as I work through hands-on rooms
and study the fundamentals — published partly to sharpen my own
understanding, and partly in case they're useful to someone walking
the same path.

My background is in self-directed technical work, including quantitative trading systems and data analysis, and
I'm now focused on building genuine, demonstrable competence in
security rather than just collecting certificates.

If you'd like to get in touch, you can reach me at kenneth@chuinwei.com or find my
work on [GitHub](https://github.com/BoardwalkLord).
